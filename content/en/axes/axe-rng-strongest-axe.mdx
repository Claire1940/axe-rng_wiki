<Section icon={Sprout} title="Rebirth Mechanics and Long-Term Luck">
Rebirthing is a critical long-term strategy in Axe RNG that directly impacts your ability to acquire the **strongest axe** over time. It offers a permanent Luck bonus, making each subsequent playthrough more efficient. Understanding when and how to rebirth is key to sustained growth.

<Callout type="info">
Rebirth is not just a reset; it's a strategic investment. The temporary loss of progress is traded for a permanent increase in Luck, which compounds over multiple rebirths.
</Callout>

The decision to rebirth should be carefully considered, balancing your current progress with the potential future gains. Don't rebirth too early, but also don't wait too long if your current run has stagnated.

### Rebirth Timing and Impact

| Timing                   | Action                                          | Benefit                                                         | Tradeoff                                                               |
| :----------------------- | :---------------------------------------------- | :-------------------------------------------------------------- | :--------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Before First Rebirth** | Push current axe, skills, bees, and tree progress until slowdown | Maximize value from the current run before resetting              | Waiting too long can slow future axe rolls and progression             |
| Good Rebirth Window      | When stronger axe rolls and zone progress slow  | **Permanent Luck increase** for future rolls                    | Temporary pause in short-term progression; need to rebuild            |
| After Rebirth            | Roll for a stronger axe first, then rebuild     | Higher Luck supports the next cycle of axe rolling and zone pushing | Early post-rebirth progress can feel slower initially                |
| Before Zone Pushing      | Stack available Luck progress before important axe rolls | Better rolls make it easier to chop stronger trees and reach later zones | Rolling without Luck preparation can waste time and resources         |
