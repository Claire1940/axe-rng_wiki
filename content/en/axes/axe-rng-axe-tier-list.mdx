<Section icon={Target} title="Optimal Roll Strategy for Axe RNG">
Efficiently rolling for axes is a core mechanic in Axe RNG. It's not just about hitting the roll button; it's about strategic timing and resource management to maximize your chances of getting high-value axes. Your roll strategy directly impacts how quickly you progress and unlock new content.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Secure Your Starter Axe">
    Before anything else, redeem the `THANKYOU` code to get the **Shiny Broad Axe**. This axe will be your primary chopping tool for the initial stages and significantly outperforms your default axe.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Farm Resources for Initial Upgrades">
    With a decent axe equipped, focus on chopping trees to gather resources. These resources are essential for skill upgrades that can indirectly boost your rolling efficiency or chopping power, preparing you for more serious rolls.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Time Your Luck Boosts">
    Save your Luck, Super Luck, and Ultra Luck Potions. These boosts are most effective when used in concentrated rolling sessions, not during casual play. Activating them when you're ready to commit to multiple rolls significantly increases your chances of a rare outcome.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Roll Until a Clear Upgrade">
    Once your Luck boosts are active, start rolling. Continue until you obtain an axe that offers a clear and substantial upgrade to your current equipped axe. A "clear upgrade" means a noticeable increase in chopping speed or a rarity tier jump.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Re-evaluate and Repeat">
    After securing a better axe, re-equip it and continue farming. Monitor your chopping speed and progression. If you notice a slowdown, it's time to gather more resources, possibly rebirth for increased base Luck, and repeat the rolling process.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Avoid Wasting Luck Boosts">
Do not use Luck potions if you are only planning a few casual rolls. These valuable resources should be reserved for dedicated rolling sessions where you can make multiple attempts to secure a significant axe upgrade. Wasting them on random button presses will slow your progression.
</Callout>
