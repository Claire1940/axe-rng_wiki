### When to Rebirth?
A general rule for this **axe rng beginner guide** is to rebirth as soon as you can no longer unlock a new skill upgrade within 10 minutes of active farming. While you lose your current axes and skills, the permanent Luck bonus makes your next run significantly faster.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Check Your Luck Multiplier">
    Before rebirthing, ensure you have claimed all one-time Luck bonuses (Group join, etc.).
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Spend Remaining Resources">
    Logs and crystals are reset upon rebirth. Spend every last one on temporary boosts or bee hatches before you hit the button.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Immediate Post-Rebirth Roll">
    After rebirthing, use your new Luck multiplier to roll for a better axe immediately. You will likely find a "Rare" or "Epic" axe within your first 5 rolls.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Rebirth Warning">
