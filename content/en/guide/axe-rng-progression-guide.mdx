Rebirthing resets your logs, skills, and zone progress, but it grants you a permanent multiplier to your **Base Luck**. This is the only way to consistently roll S-Tier and God-Tier axes in the late game.

**Zone Progression Route:**
- **Zone 1 (Starter Forest)**: Focus on the THANKYOU code axe and basic skills.
- **Zone 2 (Autumn Grove)**: First major hurdle. Requires at least 500 Power.
- **Zone 3 (Crystal Caves)**: High crystal yield. Stay here to max out early skills.
- **Zone 4 (Frozen Tundra)**: Requires high swing speed due to "Ice Bark" mechanics.
- **Zone 5 (Enchanted Wilds)**: The current endgame. Requires Mythic-tier axes.

<Callout type="warning" title="Rebirth Timing">
Never rebirth the moment you hit the requirement. Instead, push as far into your current zone as possible to collect extra crystals. These crystals are often kept through the rebirth (depending on the update version), giving you a massive head start on your next run.
</Callout>
