### Optimal Rolling Strategy

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Claim Codes First">
    Redeem all active codes, especially those granting Luck potions or a strong starting axe like the Shiny Broad Axe.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Equip Best Available">
    Always equip your most powerful axe. Even a slight increase in chopping power makes a difference.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Stack Luck Boosts">
    Save Luck, Super Luck, and Ultra Luck potions for dedicated rolling sessions. Using them one by one is less efficient.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Roll Until Significant Upgrade">
    Roll until you get an axe that visibly improves your chopping speed or allows you to clear trees in a new zone more easily.
  </Step>
</Steps>
