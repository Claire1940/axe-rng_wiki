<Section icon={Trophy} title="Rebirth Timing and Luck Optimization">

Rebirthing is the ultimate progression reset. In Axe RNG, a rebirth wipes your current axes and skills but grants a permanent increase to your **Base Luck**. Knowing when to pull the trigger is the most debated part of any **axe rng tree chopping guide**.

| Timing | Current Progress State | Rebirth Benefit |
|---|---|---|
| **Too Early** | Still clearing trees easily | Minimal luck gain, slows progress |
| **Optimal** | Zone progression has stalled | Significant luck boost for next run |
| **Too Late** | Farming for hours with no gain | Wasted time that could have been luck-boosted |

The 2026 meta suggests rebirthing when the "Luck Gain" indicator shows at least a **+5% total increase** to your account's permanent stats. Before you rebirth, ensure you have used all your current resources on any non-permanent upgrades that might grant a final achievement or small bonus.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Check Luck Threshold">
    Look at the Rebirth menu. If the luck bonus is low, continue farming the highest zone you can reach.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Burn Resources">
    Spend remaining logs on any skill that might increase your rebirth value.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Execute Rebirth">
    Confirm the reset. Your axes will be gone, but your new luck stat makes getting them back 10x faster.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Post-Rebirth Strategy">
Immediately after rebirthing, use your new luck to roll for a "Rare" or "Epic" axe. Do not settle for common tools; your increased luck should make landing a mid-tier axe nearly guaranteed within the first 20 rolls.
