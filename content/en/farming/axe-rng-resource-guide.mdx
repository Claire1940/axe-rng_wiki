Axe RNG, developed by Chopsy Games, is a Roblox incremental simulator centered around rolling for axes, chopping trees, upgrading skills, hatching bees, collecting honey, and rebirthing for increased luck. <Section icon={Swords} title="Optimizing Axe Rolls and Chopping Efficiency">
Your axe is your most important tool in Axe RNG. The game's core loop begins with rolling for better axes, which directly impacts your chopping speed and resource generation. Understanding when to roll and what axes to prioritize is fundamental to any **axe rng resource guide**.

The "THANKYOU" code provides a **Shiny Broad Axe**, which is an excellent early-game boost. After that, your focus should be on consistently rolling for higher-rarity axes. Luck boosts, obtained from codes or rebirths, significantly improve your chances of getting rare axes.

<Section icon={Shield} title="Bees, Honey, and Parallel Progression">

This part of the **axe rng resource guide** explains how to integrate bee keeping into your routine for optimal resource generation. The game explicitly encourages hatching bees and collecting honey, so ignoring this system will significantly slow your overall growth.

### Honey Collection Strategy

| Stage | Action | Why it Matters | Common Mistake to Avoid |
| :---- | :----- | :------------- | :---------------------- |
| **Early Game** | Hatch first bees after stable chopping. | Establishes passive honey income early. | Delaying bee hatching until late game. |
| **Mid Game** | Upgrade bee capacity and honey production. | Increases honey per hour, vital for certain upgrades. | Focusing solely on axes, neglecting honey. |
| **Late Game** | Maximize bee efficiency through skills. | Honey becomes a significant resource for advanced progression. | Treating bees as a secondary, optional system. |

<Section icon={Trophy} title="Rebirth Timing and Luck Maximization">
Rebirth is a powerful mechanism in Axe RNG designed to significantly boost your luck, which in turn influences the rarity and power of your axe rolls. Knowing when to rebirth and how to maximize your luck is a critical aspect of this **axe rng resource guide**. Rebirth resets some of your progress but grants a permanent luck bonus, making future runs more fruitful.

The official game description notes that joining the Chopsy Games Roblox group grants a +1 Luck bonus. This is a free and easy way to get an early edge. Save your Super Luck and Ultra Luck potions for dedicated rolling sessions immediately after a rebirth to capitalize on the increased luck.

### Rebirth and Luck Management

| Timing | Action | Benefit | Tradeoff |
| :----- | :----- | :------ | :------- |
| **Before first rebirth** | Push current axe, skills, bees, and trees until progress slows. | Maximize value from current run before reset. | Waiting too long can hinder future rolls. |
| **Good rebirth window** | Rebirth when stronger axe rolls & zone progress halt. | Increases Luck for more valuable future rolls. | Pauses short-term pushing for long-term gain. |
| **After rebirth** | Roll for a stronger axe first, then rebuild. | Higher Luck supports next cycle of progression. | Early post-rebirth feels slower until established. |
| **Before zone pushing** | Stack available Luck boosts before important rolls. | Better rolls make clearing stronger trees easier. | Rolling without Luck prep can yield weaker results. |
